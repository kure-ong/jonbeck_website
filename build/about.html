About Jon Beck

Jon Beck has been working in the Photography industry for over 15years. Jon is a professional photographer who combines precise technical skills with an approachable and personable demeanour to help you achieve your vision.

“I am drawn to the diversity of the world – from Australia’s red soil centre, the character in peoples faces and the unique textures of our building heritage. My combined passion for the outdoors , people and their environment enables me to create iconic images from Australia’s diverse landscape, New Zealand and the world.I believe photograph’s have to provoke an emotion, represent uniqueness and tell a story.”



Jon's pictures are not souvenirs, but images that help us to ‘see’ and to understand landscapes and people as art. Light literally exudes from Jon’s pictures like few others, and this luminescent enlightenment startles us with its clarity and perception

With a wide knowledge of photographic skills, Jon photographs many subjects beyond his portfolio that is seen online. You can keep up to date with David's latest projects through Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
